About VIGHE

We have established VIGHE or Institute under SSBS keeping in mind various difficulties in the field of education, the scope and importance of education etc., to enable the students of rural areas to rise to the heights of success, and also to open the doors of education at national level and international level, we have started a practice center by naming it as Asian International University, Manipur or Vidyapeetha. We are always committed to provide quality all-inclusive education to every component of the society.
